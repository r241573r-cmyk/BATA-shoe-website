About Bata Zimbabwe

For generations, Bata has been a household name in Zimbabwe – providing affordable, durable, and stylish footwear for every step of life.

Who We Are

Bata Shoe Company Zimbabwe has been serving the nation since 1939, producing quality footwear for men, women, and children. From school shoes to fashion-forward sneakers, Bata has grown into the leading shoe brand trusted by millions of Zimbabwean families.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To deliver affordable, high-quality footwear that meets the needs of every Zimbabwean, from classrooms to workplaces and beyond.

Vision

To be Zimbabwe’s most trusted and innovative footwear brand, keeping people stylish, comfortable, and confident with every step.

Values

Integrity, quality, affordability, and community service form the foundation of everything we do.

Our Heritage

Bata is a global footwear brand founded in 1894 in Zlín, Czech Republic. Since then, it has grown into one of the world’s largest shoe manufacturers with operations in more than 70 countries. Bata Zimbabwe, established over 80 years ago, continues this legacy by blending international expertise with local craftsmanship.

Community & Sustainability

We believe in giving back. From supporting local schools with durable school shoes to investing in eco-friendly production processes, Bata Zimbabwe is committed to building a better future for our communities and the environment.
